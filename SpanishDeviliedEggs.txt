Rice and sauce:
3/4 cup rice
1/3 cup margarine
1/2 cup minced onions
1/3 cup minced celery
3 tbsp flour
1 big can tomatoes
1 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
2 cloves garlic
1/4 tsp pepper

Cook rice.  Sauté onions, garlic and celery in margarine until tender. blend in flour, then tomatoes; cook, stirring, until thickened. 
Add sugar/spices. Put rice on bottom of dish, pour cauce on top, arrange deviled eggs on top and bake 5-10 minutes at 350.

Devilied Eggs:
6 hardboiled eggs. (get them to room temp. put them in covered pan with just enough water to cover the eggs. bring to boil, put heat to low and let set for 15 minutes. 

1/4 tsp worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp grated onion
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbsp mayo
1 tbsp chili
1/4 tsp cayenne

halve boiled eggs. scoop yellow out and mix with other ingredients, then spoon back into egg white.
