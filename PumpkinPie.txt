4 eggs 
1 can (798ml) pureed pumpkin
1 cup brown sugar, packed
2 tsp cinamon
1 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp salt
1.5 cups Baileys (or condensed milk)

1 10" pie shell

Preheat oven to 425 F. Mix everything up. Put it in the pie shell. Bake at 425 for 15 minutes. Lower temparature to 350 and cook for 35 minutes more.
