 INGREDIENTS
1/2 cup white sugar
2 tbsp freshly squeezed lime juice
1/4 cup fish sauce
2 tbsp tamarind pulp, made into a concentrate with a little boiling water
1 tsp shrimp paste, made into a concentrate with a little boiling water
340g dried rice noodles
1/2 cup peanut or sesame oil
1 1/2 tsp minced garlic
4 eggs
340g firm tofu, chicken, or pork cut into 2cm strips
Soy sauce
1 1/2 tbsp white sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 cup chopped unsalted, dry-roasted peanuts
1/2 cup minced pickled daikon (radish)
1/2 cup chopped fresh chives
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp cayenne pepper
2 cups fresh bean sprouts
1 lime, cut into wedges

INSTRUCTIONS
1) To prepare Pad Thai sauce: In a medium saucepan over medium heat, blend the half-cup of sugar, lime juice , fish sauce, tamarind concentrate, and shrimp paste concentrate. Remove from heat once it starts to bubble.
2) To make Pad Thai: Soak rice noodles in cold water until soft; drain. In a large skillet or wok over medium heat, warm oil and add garlic and eggs; scramble the eggs. Add your protein, stir until well mixed, and lightly brown the protein. Add noodles, a little soy sauce for colouring, and stir until noodles and protein are fully cooked.
3) Stir in Pad Thai sauce, 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar and 1 1/2 teaspoons salt. Stir in peanuts and minced radish. Remove from heat and add chives, chili powder, & cayenne.
4) Serve with lime and bean sprouts on the side.
