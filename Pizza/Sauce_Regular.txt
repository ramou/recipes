-2 tbsp olive oil
-3 tsp oregano
-1 tbsp basil
-1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
-Can of tomatoes crushed
-1/2 tsp worstechershire sauce
-1 tbsp sugar
-1/4 tsp cinamon

1. Heat the oil on mediunm till fragrant.
2. Put in oregano, basil, red pepper and stir constantly till you can smell the spices (color should change slightly).
3. Add the crushed tomatoes and worstechershire, then stir regularly for about five minutes.
4. Add the sugar and cinnamon and stir for another five minutes.
5. Switch to low heat/simmer and leave simmering, uncovered, for 20-30 minutes.
6. Take off heat and let cool.

Letting it sit in the fridge for a few days before using will improve flavor.

This makes enough for two or three 12/12.5 inch pizzas.
