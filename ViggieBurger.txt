
scale, rounding to zuchini

3.0 cups grated zuchini
2.0 cup cornmeal
1.0 large onion, finely chopped
1.0 cup frozen corn
1.0 cup black beans
1.0 cloves garlic
0.5 cups chopped cloves/green onion

1 cup fresh basil
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2 tbsp vegetable seasoning


Blend all (except half the basil/salt/pepper) ingredients but half the zuchini in a blender on pulse.

fry in oil over medium heat until it begins to soften and thickent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the remaining grated zuchini unil it begins to soften and become transluscent, another 3-4 minutes.

Mix in leftover salt, pepper and basil while in a bowl. Form into patties nad let set in freezer for 20 minutes. Cook on bbq on high heat until browned.
