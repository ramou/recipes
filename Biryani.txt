1 lb chicken, torn and pre-cooked (roughly two cups)
4 onions, diced
4 cloves garlic, diced
4 tbsp ground cumin
2 tsp ground corriander
2 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp cardamon
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp cloves
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/2 cup oil (probably olive)
2 cups chicken stock
1.5 cups rice (uncooked)
1/4 tsp salt

1. Saute onions, garlic, oil until translucent
2. Add all spices and saute until fragrant
3. Add remaining ingredients and pressure/cook
