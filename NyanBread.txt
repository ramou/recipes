Ingredients:
4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
1.5 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
1 cup warm milk - substitute almond milk
1/2 cup yogurt - substitute almond yogurt
2 tsp quick yeast
1/4 cup warm water
2 tbsp melted butter - substitute olive oil (possibly soaked in a few cloves of crushed garlic)

Steps:

1) Mix sugar and warm water.
2) When sugar is well-disolved, add yeast.
3) While yeast is becoming active and foaming, combine flour and salt in a large bowl.
4) Mix the milk/yogurt into the active/foamed yeast thoroughly
4.b) Optionally, if you have the crused garlic leftover strained from the oil, mix that into the milk/yougurt first
5) Add wet ingredients into the dry ones. Knead until smooth.
6) Cover the dough and let it rest for 1-2 hours.
7) Divide dough into balls.
8) Roll each ball into a flat oval shape.
9) Your BBQ or oven with pizza stone to max heat.
10) Place naan on stone, cook each side for 2-3 mins until puffed and golden.
11) Brush with melted butter.


BBQ would provide a more authentic char, but the oven will also work well.
