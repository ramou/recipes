Ingredients:
4 cups all-purpose flour
1.5 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
1 cup warm milk - substitute almond milk
1/2 cup yogurt - substitute almond yogurt
2 tsp quick yeast
1/4 cup warm water
2 tbsp melted butter - substitute olive oil (possibly soaked in a few cloves of crushed garlic)

Steps:

1) Mix sugar and warm water.
2) In a large bowl, combine flour and salt and yeast.
3) In another bowl, mix warm milk and yogurt.
4) Add wet ingredients into the dry ones. Knead until smooth.
5) Cover the dough and let it rest for 1-2 hours.
6) Divide dough into balls.
7) Roll each ball into a flat oval shape.
8) Your BBQ or oven with pizza stone to max heat.
9) Place naan on stone, cook each side for 2-3 mins until puffed and golden.
10) Brush with melted butter.


BBQ would provide a more authentic char, but the oven will also work well.
