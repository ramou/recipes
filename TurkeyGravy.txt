(flour does not scale with meat broth...)

-Heat to boiling 1.5 cups meat broth. 
-Stir together cold milk, broth and 4tsp flour (total of half cup liquid) until flour is undumplinged
-Take broth away from heat, put thickening in slowly, stirring a lot.
-Add 1 tsp salt. 
-Add 1/4 tsp black pepper.
-Add a pinch of herbs (basil, sage, thyme, 1/2 tsp of each) (with beef, use marjorim, rosemary, savory, basil)


Simmer gravy at least 10 minutes.
